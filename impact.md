## Digital Education

I filmed, edited, and animated a series of videos aimed at presenting linguistic theory in a clear way.  This work, which presented teaching by Prof Caroline Heycock and Prof Geoffrey K Pullum, was accomplished with the help of a series of grants from the PPLS Teaching and Learning Initiative Fund.

These videos have been posted to the official YouTube channel for Linguistics and English Language at the University of Edinburgh, where they have been viewed over 875,000 times as of March 2026.
